RESEARCH INTERESTS

The high processing power and the storage capacity of the modern computer has revolutionized the understanding of matter. The modern calculation techniques can be used to predict the equilibrium and transport properties of systems ranging from molecular scale to the bulk with very high accuracy. I evelop theoretical techniques to calculate and predict transport and physical properties of materials that has technological and scienti_c signi_cance.Such a theoretical guidance is crucial in e_cient design of experiments and technological applications

My primary research interest are thermal and electronic transport in nano scale systems and interfaces, thermoelectric generations, crystal growth and nano composites. My current research areas are on electron-phonon interactions in strained graphene interfaces, nano composites and crystal growth(Metallic Systems)
